About Us

Welcome to JobHub, the world's largest professional network with more than 1 billion members in more than 200 countries and territories worldwide.

Vision

Create economic opportunity for every member of the global workforce.

Mission

Mission of JobHub is simple: connect the world’s professionals to make them more productive and successful.



Who are we?


JobHub began in co-founder Marc Nakhoul's living room in 2002 and was officially launched on May 5, 2003. Today, JobHub leads a diversified business with revenues from membership subscriptions, advertising sales and recruitment solutions under the leadership of John Ghanem. In December 2016, Microsoft completed its acquisition of JobHub, bringing together the world’s leading professional cloud and the world’s leading professional network.
